Bio

Professor Marjan Sirjani is a researcher in formal methods, software engineering, and actor-based modeling and verification. Her work spans modeling of cyber-physical systems, trustworthy software engineering, and formal verification.

Research

Research interests include formal methods, model checking, software engineering, and modeling of cyber-physical and real-time systems. Her research focuses on actor-based modeling, trustworthy systems, and verification of safety-critical applications.
